Our founder

A gap in the system.
We decided to close it.

After working within the healthcare system and watching families struggle to find the right level of care for aging loved ones, the founder of RR Care recognized a critical gap: families needed a trusted guide — someone who understood the system from the inside, spoke plainly, and had no financial incentive to push one option over another.

RR Care was built to be that guide. We work directly for families, not facilities. We don't take referral fees. We give you the honest picture — what's available, what fits your situation, and what to watch out for.

Based in El Lago, TX and rooted in the Greater Houston community, we know the local landscape: the facilities, the home care agencies, the hospice providers. When you call us, you're talking to someone who knows your area and has relationships in it.

What makes us different

Four commitments to every family

01

Deep Local Knowledge

We know the Houston Bay Area care landscape — the good facilities, the gaps, the agencies worth trusting. That local insight is something no national directory can replicate.

02

Objective Assessments

We don't earn referral commissions. Our recommendations are based on your loved one's actual needs, not on which facility pays us more.

03

Direct Communication

You get straight answers — not jargon, not a sales pitch. We explain your options clearly so you can make the decision that's right for your family.

04

Strict Compliance

We operate within all state and federal care regulations, and we help families understand their rights and the standards that facilities are held to.
